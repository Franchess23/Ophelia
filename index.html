At the top of the Beekman Tower,

A 360° greenhouse terrace, there is a luxurious rooftop restaurant dishing up delectable cuisine, all with breathtaking city views. This revered example of traditional New York architecture, created by John Mead Howells with sculptures by Rene Paul Chambellan, is topped by a statue of Ophelia. New Yorkers’ muse, including former regular Frank Sinatra. You can now enjoy it. All have access to the 26th floor now, and a new generation is being inspired as a result.
